Klik BCA

  1. Click the "Top Up" button below;
  2. Choose KlikBCA as your payment method;
  3. Fill in the required fields; your amount of Top Up and KlikBCA ID;
  4. Log in to KlikBCA website;
  5. Go to menu " e-Commerce Payments " and select the category "Others" and then select "Indomog;
  6. Confirm your SITTI Top Up transaction;
  7. You will get an Confirmation SMS / Email from KlikBCA;
  8. The balance will appear on your account;
  9. If you don't do the confirmation in the next 60 minutes in the KlikBCA website, the TOP UP request form the SITTI website would be canceled;
  10. Should you have any problem with your Top Up process, please send an email to billing@SITTI.co.id. Please include the Transaction Code in the Email.

 

SMS Banking Mandiri

  1. Click the "Top Up" button below;
  2. Choose SMS Banking Mandiri as your payment method;
  3. Fill in the required fields; number of Top Up and cell phone numbers
  4. Next, follow the instructions you get from a mobile phone top-up to complete the process.;
  5. Should you have any problem with your Top Up process, please send an email to billing@SITTI.co.id. Please include the Transaction Code in the Email.

 

Internet Banking Mandiri

  1. Click the "Top Up" button below;
  2. Choose Internet Bank Mandiri as your payment method;
  3. Fill in the required fields; your amount of Top Up and your ATM card number;
  4. To proceed, use your token, and enter the following requirements into your token: the last 10 digits of your ATM card number and the amount of TOP UP with a minimum of Rp 10,000, -;
  5. After entering all the requirements into the token, enter the number that has been generated from the token into the last field on the web and press "Continue";
  6. Your balance in SITTI would be updated. If nothing happens in 30 minutes, please contact billing@sitti.co.id.

 

Voucher Topup SITTI

  1. Enter the code shown on the SITTI Voucher
  2. Your account will be filled with the same amount of your voucher
  3. A Voucher can only be used once.
