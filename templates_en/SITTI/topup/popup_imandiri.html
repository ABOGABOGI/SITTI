Internet Banking Mandiri

  1. Click the "Top Up" button below;
  2. Choose Internet Bank Mandiri as your payment method;
  3. Fill in the required fields; your amount of Top Up and your ATM card number;
  4. To proceed, use your token, and enter the following requirements into your token: the last 10 digits of your ATM card number and the amount of TOP UP with a minimum of Rp 10,000, -;
  5. After entering all the requirements into the token, enter the number that has been generated from the token into the last field on the web and press "Continue";
  6. Your balance in SITTI would be updated. If nothing happens in 30 minutes, please contact billing@sitti.co.id.
