Klik BCA

  1. Click the "Top Up" button below;
  2. Choose KlikBCA as your payment method;
  3. Fill in the required fields; your amount of Top Up and KlikBCA ID;
  4. Log in to KlikBCA website;
  5. Go to menu " e-Commerce Payments " and select the category "Others" and then select "Indomog;
  6. Confirm your SITTI Top Up transaction;
  7. You will get an Confirmation SMS / Email from KlikBCA;
  8. The balance will appear on your account;
  9. If you don't do the confirmation in the next 60 minutes in the KlikBCA website, the TOP UP request form the SITTI website would be canceled;
  10. Should you have any problem with your Top Up process, please send an email to billing@SITTI.co.id. Please include the Transaction Code in the Email.
